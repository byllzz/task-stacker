How to use:
  • Change your mood by tapping on the screen or using the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard.
  • Press theStartbutton to begin using TaskStacker.
  • Click the Add Task button or the + button at the bottom to create a new task.
  • Type your task and confirm to save it to your list.
  • Mark tasks as complete once you finish them.
  • Use this app daily to stay organized and focused.
